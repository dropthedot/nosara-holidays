Luxury Services

We believe luxury means presence and care, not excess. The private services we offer, in-home massage and bodywork, private yoga instruction, and personal chef experiences, are all provided by trusted practitioners we know personally. They are people we have chosen because of their skill set, and the quality of feeling they bring into your space. Each experience is designed to be intimate and personal, the kind of service we invite into our own home.
